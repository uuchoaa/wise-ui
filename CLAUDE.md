## Conventions

- Code, comments, filenames, docs, and UI copy: **English**.
- Money: USD, formatted `$10,560.00`. No cents when whole unless the surrounding context (invoice total, line item) normally shows them.
- Time: relative (`just now`, `2h ago`, `yesterday`); fall back to `Jan 31, 2023` beyond a week.
- NEVER co-sign any commit.
